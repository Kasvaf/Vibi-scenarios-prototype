## Conversation Highlights

### What Made This Natural:

1. **Balanced Perspective**: Acknowledged tourist attractions while adding local insight

2. **Personalized Filtering**: Asked about interests, then tailored everything to food/adventure

3. **Honest Opinions**: Admitted when something is touristy but explained why it's still worth it

4. **Practical Guidance**: Included pricing, timing, booking tips throughout

5. **Progressive Building**: Answered questions as they came up naturally, not all at once

6. **Insider Knowledge**: Premium vs budget options, local spots, timing tips

### Information Architecture:

Vibi organized recommendations by:
- **Must-See**: Burj Khalifa, Desert Safari
- **Food**: Old Dubai crawl (specific spots)
- **Beach**: Multiple options (premium vs free)
- **Nightlife**: Rooftop + casual venues
- **Day Trip**: Abu Dhabi with specific plan

All delivered conversationally, not as a list.
